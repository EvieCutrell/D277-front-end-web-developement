The most popular city in the state: Seattle!

The hard facts of Seattle

City population: 749,256.
Year city was incorporated: 1865 and was unincorporated in 1867. Later reincorporated in 1869.
Region of the state in which city is located: West of the Pudget Sound and East of Lake Washington
City Classification: Urban.
Average income of city vs state: Seattle's average household income is $116,068 compared to the state's average of $89,138
