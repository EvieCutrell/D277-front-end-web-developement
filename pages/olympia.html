All about the state capital: Olympia!

The hard facts of Olympia

City population: 55,669.
Year city was incorporated: 1859 as a town, later in 1882 as a city.
Region of the state in which city is located: Southern edge of the Pudget Sound
City Classification: Often considered Urban-Suburban mix.
Average income of city vs state: Olympia's average household income is $73,850 compared to the state's average of $89,138
